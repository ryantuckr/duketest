Point Collector

You will be gicen a random number at the start of the game.

There are four Duke players shooting below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The vaule of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
